The Little Crayons

Our Classes

At The Crayons School, we offer a wide range of classes for children to explore, learn, and grow. Here are the details of each class:

Nursery

A fun and engaging environment for toddlers to start their learning journey through games, music, and storytelling.

KG1

KG1 helps children develop basic skills in literacy, numeracy, and social interactions with a focus on hands-on learning.

KG2

In KG2, children build on their foundational skills and are introduced to more advanced learning activities and creative exploration.
